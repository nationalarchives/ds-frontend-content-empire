Extracts from the accounts of the British East India Company, 1678
(Catalogue ref: E 140/9/4 f42)
Source 2a

Franco de la Cruz to Adventure to Manilla for goods sould & retorned back viz:  
   
for 321 barrs Iron g t pi: 106: 75 cattle at £6  
piece abating 18pound for frt & Comission &on 538: 06  
for 4 paire silke stockings sould for 16 –  
for 2 chests of wines abating  
to Manilla & frt back of 2 chests  
returned to Bantam ntt amounting too 47 –  
for 1 barrell of brandy sould for but 8 –  
  £609: 06

Source 2b

2 silver candlesticks qt 45 ¾ 45: 45
